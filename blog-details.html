A Glimpse of My Experience with Insight Club

Truthfully, the Insight Club, more like the Insight family, was the highlight of my university years. It gave me a sense of belonging that no other community has ever given me. Meeting people with the same purpose, ideals, morals, and beliefs as mine empowered me to not let go of them in a time and environment where they are on the verge of becoming extinct.

Not to mention the events and activities that tightened our bond, from the Charity Event, the iftar we shared at the End of year Gathering, the Ramadan event and decorations that brightened up the AUB campus, to the hiking trip that we enjoyed greatly. This is just a glimpse of my experience that no enough words can capture.

The Insight Club is blessing that I’m immensely grateful for 🤍

Each one of us is on their own journey of faith, for me Insight has done nothing but push me further on that journey. Knowing that I have the support of this family, I gained the strength to put my prayer mat anywhere, on campus and outside campus, with no hesitation and fear of judgment. I learned to earn my right to communicate with God anytime, anywhere.

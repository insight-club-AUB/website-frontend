About Us

The Insight Club is an independent student club at the American University of Beirut (AUB). Our mission is to provide perspectives on a well-rounded way of living and appreciating life through balancing between physical, intellectual, and spiritual needs. Our events include charity projects, cultural celebrations, and intellectual activities. We aim to provide our members with opportunities to grow and with new dimensions to the diverse AUB experience. We develop our members in various aspects of life. Social, Physical, Academic, Spiritual, and Intellectual.

What does the communication team do?

  • They invite new people to be part of the insight family.
  • They organise events.
  • They help in the decision making process concerning big events.
  • They are responsible for parts of the events (usually activities part).

What does Newsletter team do?

  • At the end of every week, unique newsletter full of insight Islamic spirit is sent
  • It includes a weekly motivation, Hadith, Duaa and video about a specific theme
  • Summary of the club events with memorable pictures
  • Announcement of the upcoming events with needed details

Insight Tutoring Program

Serving the purpose of mutual support and improving our members’ academic performance, the Insight Club provides tutoring sessions in diverse courses from different majors. Club members actively work and coordinate to provide educational aid through various resources, documents, lectures, notes, previouses, etc… Tutoring sessions take place inside AUB and in accordance to the participant’s availability.

Insight's Club 40 Year Anniversary

The Insight Club was formed 40 years ago by Muslim alumnus for the sole purpose of creating an Islamic environment inside AUB to support the Muslim community. Through various social, intellectual, spiritual, academic, and physical activities, the club managed to host many students and grow its community. Over the years, the Insight Club became one of the most popular and active clubs at AUB. Currently, it is the biggest community in AUB with more than 300 members Elhamdoulilah.

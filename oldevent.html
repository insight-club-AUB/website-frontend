Insight Club's Charity Event

  • Feb 13, 2022

قال ﷺ: لا يؤمن أحدكم حتى يحب لأخيه ما يحب لنفسه
Following the commandments of our prophet (PBUH) and for the purpose of demonstrating the Islamic virtues, the Insight Club organized one of its biggest charity events in the spring of 2022. The event targeted the Syrian refugees living in Al Yasmine Camp in Beqaa.

Under the title “من القلب للقلب” – From the Heart to the Heart - the Insight Club members volunteered in packaging 220 wool clothes packages, 220 gifts, and 500 bags of snacks for kids. At a later stage, they visited the camp where the distribution of the packages took place, and recreational activities were conducted for kids.
This event was an inspiration for many and gratefully helped the refugees.

Insight's Club 41 Year Anniversary

The Insight Club was formed 41 years ago by Muslim alumnus for the sole purpose of creating an Islamic environment inside AUB to support the Muslim community. Through various social, intellectual, spiritual, academic, and physical activities, the club managed to host many students and grow its community. Over the years, the Insight Club became one of the most popular and active clubs at AUB. Currently, it is the biggest community in AUB with more than 300 members Elhamdoulilah.
